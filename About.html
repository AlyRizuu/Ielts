OVERVIEW

We believe that the modern academic and professional landscape is more competitive than it has ever been. At the same time, the world is in need of creative, innovative individuals who have both intellectual and emotional intelligence, as well as a desire to make a difference. At VISION TECH, we believe in providing you with the guidance you need to have a balanced, self-aware outlook and find your true potential in order to add value to your own life and create long-term, sustainable impact in your communities.

OBJECTIVES

  • IELTS For UKVI(CBT)
  • IELTS For UKVI Life Skills
  • GRE/SAT Preparatory Classes
  • English Foundation Course
  • Career Counselling

SOCIAL DEVELOPMENT

Vision Tech Education firmly believes that for the development of a sustainable future, our younger generations must have the capacity, conceptual foresight and global perspectives in regards to the challenges that face our country from economic, cultural, social perspectives. Thus, we have conducted campaigns and events that are aimed towards holistic development of social awareness and effective solutions.

CAREER GUIDANCE

Specially designed aptitude tests are utilized to assess the ideal career pathways for younger generations that may not at this point be aware of their full potential. Using our tailored and specialized assessment program we will determine not just the needs of the individual but also the most impactful pathways towards achieving their goals and the best methodologies to apply towards gaining the toolkit, whether in terms of personality, mental processing, academics, professional opportunities or communal interaction that would best benefit them and the communities they represent.

INTELLECTUAL DEVELOPMENT

We believe that mindset development via experiential opportunities is an imperative focus of personal and holistic growth for our human capital. Intellectual value is not simply gained via traditional learning techniques using books and classroom methodologies, but is also augmented through the values gained in practical experiences. Kestrel Education uses the avenues of research, volunteerism, internships and summer programs to provide another layer of awareness and development that can be further applied in personal, professional and social efforts by future generations

MENTORSHIP

We believe that personal growth is greater than the sum total of academic and professional accomplishments, and takes place through the expansion of the intellectual, creative and socially conscious mental and emotional horizons of each individual.
