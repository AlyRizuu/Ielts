WHY DO YOU NEED CAREER COUNSELLING IN GILGIT-BALTISTAN ?

LACK OF CLARITY

Many students face the dilemma of selecting the right career due to lack of clarity about their interests, aptitudes and skills.

PARENTAL PRESSURE

Parents sometimes force their children to opt for a particular career choice based on their preferences and financial prospects, which may not align with the students’ interests and aspirations.

LIMITED EXPOSURE

Some students may not have enough exposure to the available career options due to the limited availability of job-related information and guidance.

MARKET DEMAND

Students often struggle with the decision of pursuing their desired career due to the limited job opportunities or low demand in the market.

FINANCIAL CONSTRAINTS

The cost of education and training for certain careers may be high, making it challenging for some students to follow their preferred career path.

FEAR OF FAILURE

The fear of failure or not being successful in their chosen career often prevents students from committing to a career path, resulting in indecision and confusion.
